## About me

I'm a frontend-focused web developer who enjoys building clean, intuitive interfaces.
I work mainly with Angular and TypeScript, and occasionally dive into the backend with ASP.NET Core.
Outside of code, I'm into art — it influences the way I think about design and UI.
